Slipstream

An open program to design a modular starship and orbital dockyard—propulsion, structures, avionics, GNC, thermal, power, life support, and payload.

Get Involved Docs

Mission

Build an openly verifiable spaceflight stack. Specifications, CAD, simulation models, tests, and flight results are public by design.

  • Open designs under permissive licensing
  • Interfaces first: versioned subsystem contracts
  • Validated sims, reproducible rigs, real test data
  • Safety culture, documentation, and peer review

Subsystems

Propulsion

Engine cycles, feed systems, pressurization, and ground/flight test plans with CFD/FEM-backed iteration.

Avionics

Radiation-tolerant computing, deterministic buses, and flight software you can build locally.

Structures

Mass‑efficient primary/secondary structure with manufacturability constraints and open tooling.

GNC

Actuators, sensors, filters, and guidance modes with HIL benches and public logs.

Thermal & Power

Thermal control, TPS, and power generation/storage with clear derating and margins.

Life Support & Payload

Closed‑loop ECLSS options and adaptable payload interfaces for research and ops.

Governance

Slipstream uses a merit‑driven, review‑heavy model. Work is tracked in public repos with RFCs, issues, and milestones.

  • RFCs for major changes
  • Codeowners & subsystem leads
  • Design reviews & test readiness reviews
  • Safety board & incident postmortems
